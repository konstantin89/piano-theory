Short keys
==========

Ctrl+D - deselect all notes

Quick Quantize
==============
Helps to align MIDI notes to time pattern.

* Go to the piano screen of the pattern you want to quantize.
* Press Ctrl+A to select all notes.
* Press Ctrl+Q to quantize (Or go to tools -> Quick Quantize).
